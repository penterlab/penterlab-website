Aleksandra Andrzejewska is a medical student who is investigating clinical presentations and therapeutic 
trajectories of extramedullary acute myeloid leukemia (eAML). She is co-supervised by Jana Ihlow and Livius Penter.
She will leverage the rich clinical data of the AML and transplant program at Charité - Universitätsmedizin Berlin.
