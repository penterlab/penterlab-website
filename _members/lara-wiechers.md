Lara is a clinician-scientist interested in clonal dynamics in cancer. 
Her work applies single-cell genomics and transcriptomics to uncover how clonal evolution 
drives therapeutic resistance and relapse in acute myeloid leukemia. 
She holds an MD from Hannover Medical School and is currently completing her specialist training in Hematology and Oncology.
