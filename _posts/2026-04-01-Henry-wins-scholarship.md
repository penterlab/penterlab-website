Henry Tietz has won the prestigious [Mildred-Scheel scholarship](https://www.krebshilfe.de/forschen/foerderung/foerderprogramme/nachwuchsfoerderung/mildred-scheel-doktoranden/) 
by Deutsche Krebshilfe (German Cancer Aid) that will support 12 months of his medical thesis in which he will identify leukemia-specific TCRs. Congratulations!
