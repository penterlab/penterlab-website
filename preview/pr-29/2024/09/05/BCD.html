We show co-evolution of mitochondrial and somatic nuclear DNA mutations using various approaches including reanalysis of bulk RNA and WES-seq data, single cell DNA sequencing and ASAP-seq. Further, we use maternal mitochondrial DNA variants to track rare donor or recipient-derived single cells following allogeneic stem cell transplantation to map out microchimeric states, such as residual leukemia at incipient relapse or remaining donor-derived hematopoiesis at time of frank hematologic recurrence.
